Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are the one of my favorite foods. Here is a reciepe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingrediants

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20seconds (as if your are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
